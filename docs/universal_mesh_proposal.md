**SUBJECT:** A Proposal to Unite the Global Digital Commons: The Creation of a Free, Resilient, and People-Owned Internet Utility.

## INTRODUCTION: The End of the Digital Dark Ages

The Internet—the single most powerful tool for human connection and progress—is failing. It is defined by rising costs, centralized control, vulnerability to global failure, and the exclusion of billions. This is not a technical necessity; it is a failure of governance.

We propose the **Universal Mesh Network (UMN)**, or Internet 2.0, a new global digital infrastructure. The UMN is a self-governing utility built on the principle that connectivity must be free, fundamental, and secured by integrity, not by gatekeepers.

The UMN is not a replacement; it is an upgrade. It harmonizes every existing connection—fiber, 5G, Wi-Fi, satellite—into a single, resilient, and heterogeneous network.

## I. THE COVENANT OF OWNERSHIP AND GOVERNANCE: A New Social Contract

The stability of the UMN rests on transferring control from centralized corporations to the distributed power of humanity, managed by auditable algorithms.

### A. Ownership by the People (The DAO Mandate)

**Public Ownership:** The network's value and protocols will be held by a publicly accountable, legally recognized Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO). The DAO serves as the universal steward of the network's future.

**The Universal Infrastructure Dividend (UID):** We replace subscription fees with compensation. Every individual who participates—allowing their device to serve as a micro-router—earns Governance Tokens and a lifetime UID. Network use becomes an asset, not a cost.

**Anti-Monopoly Guarantee:** The DAO's governance enforces a Centralization Tax and a hard Voting Cap on token ownership. This is a non-negotiable principle, ensuring no single entity—corporate or governmental—can ever gain majority control.

### B. Algorithmic Stewardship (The Hivemind AI)

Network operation will be managed by a Hivemind AI, a distributed intelligence utilizing Federated Learning across billions of nodes.

**Mandate:** The AI is permanently sworn to maximize resilience, minimize latency, and enforce the digital Bill of Rights (Privacy, Access, Neutrality).

**Accountability:** The AI's decision-making process is not a black box. It operates under a Zero-Trust security framework and constantly publishes Self-Auditing Proofs to assure the public that its operation is transparent, fair, and free of bias.

## IV. THE FINANCIAL MANDATE: Decoupling Capital from Control

The UMN is funded by its future utility, separating financial investment from democratic governance. The source of capital must never grant undue voting power.

**Egalitarian Funding:** Initial capital is sourced through a Fair Launch Token Generation Event, with funding immediately directed to the DAO's Initial Treasury. This Treasury's first duty is to fund the Proof of Personhood (PoP) cryptographic challenge, establishing the one-person, one-vote principle as financially viable.

**Infrastructure Bonds:** The DAO will issue Infrastructure Bonds that allow global capital (institutional or individual) to invest in specific regional Gateway deployments. These bonds offer a fixed, non-voting financial return, ensuring that necessary funding is secured without violating the principle of identity-weighted governance.

**Utility Monetization:** The DAO generates recurring revenue by selling bulk data transit rights to large content providers and cloud services, utilizing the decentralized backbone. This revenue stream directly funds the Universal Infrastructure Dividend (UID) paid to all mesh contributors, making the network self-sustaining.
